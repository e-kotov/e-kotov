I am a Spatial Data Scientist and **[Doctoral Researcher](https://www.demogr.mpg.de/en/about_us_6113/staff_directory_1899/egor_kotov_4279/)** at the Department of Digital and Computational Demography, [Laboratory of Migration and Mobility](https://www.demogr.mpg.de/en/research_6120/digital_and_computational_demography_zagheni_11666/migration_and_mobility_11669/) at the [Max Planck Institute for Demographic Research (MPIDR)](https://www.demogr.mpg.de/) and **[Sociodemography Research Group](https://www.upf.edu/web/demosoc)** at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF). I also serve as a computation and reproducibility co-editor at the [Population Dynamics Lab](https://population-dynamics-lab.csde.washington.edu/).

I focus on analysis of digital trace data to study human mobility patterns and its effects on disease dynamics and spatial inequalities.

#### [`{spanishoddata}`](https://github.com/rOpenSpain/spanishoddata)
**Access Spanish origin-destination matrices.**
Access Spanish origin-destination matrices based on aggregated mobile phone based human mobility data. Featured by the [Ministry of Transport and Sustainable Mobility of Spain](https://www.transportes.gob.es/ministerio/proyectos-singulares/estudios-de-movilidad-con-big-data/opendata-movilidad). In collaboration with [Robin Lovelace](https://www.robinlovelace.net/) and [Eugeni Vidal-Tortosa](https://orcid.org/0000-0001-5199-4103).
